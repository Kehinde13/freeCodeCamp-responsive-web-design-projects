Here's a time line of Baba Fela's life:

  • 1938 - Born Olufela Olusegun Oludotun Ransome-Kuti in Abeokuta, Nigeria
  • 1946 - Fela begins learning the piano, encouraged by his father, who believes studying music is an essential prt of a good education.
  • 1958 - Fela leaves Abeokuta for London. His parent Hoped he would study medcine but Fela is determined to continue with music.
  • 1959 - Fela forms his first group, Fela and his Rakers. The band record four sides for melodisc. one of the first britain's first independent African and Caribbean music labels.
  • 1961 - Fela marries Remi, His first daughter Yeni is born.
  • 1962 - Fela graduates from Trinity college of music. his first son, Femi is born
  • 1964 - NBC hires Fela as a junior producer. Drummer Tony Allen joins his band.
  • 1965 - Fela is fired forms a second editon of Koola Lobitos.
  • 1968 - Fela's first album, a compilation of Koola Lobitos singles, is released.
  • 1970 - Fela and Nigeria 70 record ten tracks in Los Angeles, later released in Nigeria as Fela Fela Fela.
  • 1971 - Fela chnages his band's name from Nigeria 70 to Africa 70.
  • 1973 - Fela and Africd 70 release Gentleman thefirst fully formed Afrobeat album.
  • 1974 - Fela is wrongfully jailed giving him material for future albums Alabgon close and Expensive Shit.
  • 1976 - Fela increases his involvement with the direct-action political movement the oung African pioneers.
  • 1977 - in an unprecedentedly brutal soldiers batter their way into kalakuta and raze it to the ground.
  • 1981 - Fela renames Afrika 70 as Egypt 80 and tours Europe
  • 1984 - Fela is arrested and has to cancel an upcoming tour.
  • 1986 - Fela is frred from jail. He announces that he will run for president.
  • 1989 - Fela an Egypt 80 resume international touring, including two shows in New York with James Brown.
